Arroz con huevo

Boiled rice with fried eggs

This dish is made whitin 20 minutes, the rices is a regular recipe made boiling it with water and a little bit of salt.
The eggs should be fried as much as you want and at the end you can also put some more salt on top of the egg.
Now just enjoy not starving.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Cook te rice.
  2. fry the eggs.
  3. serve both in a plate.
  4. add some salt if you want.
  5. Done! Enjoy
