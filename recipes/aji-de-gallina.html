Aji de Gallina

shereded chicken with rice and potatoes

This dish is made whitin 40 minutes, the rice is a regular recipe made boiling it with water and a little bit of salt.
The chicken should be boiled too and then shereded.
The sauce is made using a yellow pepper from Peru mixed with some other stuff.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Chicken
  • Yellow Pepper
  • Other stuff

Steps:

  1. Cook te rice.
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Cook the sauce
  4. Mix everything
  5. Done! Enjoy
